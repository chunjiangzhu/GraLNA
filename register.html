Registration

Interested in joining the cohort of 2025?

To receive full consideration for the program, apply online here January 5, 2025 – March 15, 2025. A completed application consists of the following:

  • A personal statement that describes your expectation on the REU and highlights your past achievements and plan after graduation
  • Resume
  • Online application, which includes providing 2 references
  • Official or unofficial transcript from student’s current institution. If your school offers it, electronic transcripts will be accepted.

If you have any questions about the application, please email Dr. Chunjiang Zhu at chunjiang.zhu@uncg.edu or Dr. Qianqian Tong at q_tong@uncg.edu.
